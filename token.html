Frequently Asked Questions

What blockchain is EXIAL on?+
EXIAL is an ERC-20 token deployed on the Polygon network. Polygon offers fast, low-cost transactions — making it ideal for frequent payments on the EXIAL platform. You can bridge to Ethereum mainnet using official Polygon bridges.
What is the token price?+
The Phase 1 presale price is $0.0050 per EXIAL. The price increases with each phase. At launch on DEX exchanges, we anticipate a listing price of $0.0150 — a 3x from presale. Price is subject to market conditions.
What is the total supply?+
The hard cap is 2,000,000,000 (2 billion) EXIAL. This is enforced in the smart contract and can never be exceeded. Unsold presale tokens will be burned or added to the ecosystem fund by DAO vote.
When will EXIAL be listed on exchanges?+
We plan to list on decentralized exchanges (DEX) via QuickSwap on Polygon in Q3 2026, alongside our platform launch. CEX listings are targeted for Phase 3 in Q1 2027.
Is there a vesting period?+
Presale tokens are unlocked at TGE (Token Generation Event). Team and advisor tokens vest over 24 months with a 6-month cliff, enforced via the EXIALTimelock smart contract.
Has the smart contract been audited?+
Our contracts are built on OpenZeppelin's industry-standard ERC-20 library and are currently undergoing a third-party security audit. Audit results will be published publicly before the public sale goes live.
Can I earn EXIAL without buying?+
Yes! Every purchase made on the EXIAL platform via Stripe earns you EXIAL rewards — automatically minted to your connected wallet through the EXIALRewards contract. No investment required.
